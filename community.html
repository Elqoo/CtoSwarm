Frequently Asked Questions

What is the expected time commitment?

CTO Swarm is designed for busy leaders. Most members engage 30–60 minutes per week — browsing discussions, attending the monthly events that interest them, and dipping into the knowledge base when needed. There's no minimum requirement; you get out what you put in.

How does the 1-month free trial work?

When you join as a paid member, your first month is completely free — no credit card required to get started. After the trial period you can decide whether to continue or cancel with no obligation and no questions asked.

Who is this community really for?

CTO Swarm is exclusively for practicing CTOs and VP/Head of Engineering at startups and scale-ups. We vet every application to ensure a high-quality, peer-only environment — no consultants, salespeople, or recruiters. If you're actively leading engineering and dealing with the real challenges that come with it, this community is for you.

What is the cancellation and renewal policy?

You can cancel your membership at any time from your account settings. If you cancel, you'll retain full access until the end of your current billing period. Memberships auto-renew unless cancelled. We don't offer refunds for partial billing periods.
